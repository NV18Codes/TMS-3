Frequently Asked Questions

How Can We Assist You?

What types of materials do you supply?
We supply a wide range of materials, including construction materials, tools, hardware, and industrial supplies to meet all project needs.
Do you provide delivery services?
Yes, we offer reliable delivery services to ensure materials reach your site on time. Delivery charges may vary based on location.
Can I place a bulk order for a discount?
Yes, we offer bulk order discounts depending on the quantity and type of materials. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
What are your payment options?
We accept multiple payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payment systems for your convenience.
Do you offer credit facilities for businesses?
Yes, we provide credit facilities to registered businesses based on eligibility and terms. Please contact us to learn more.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is placed, we provide tracking details via email or SMS. You can also contact our support team for updates.
What is your return and refund policy?
Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unused items in their original condition. Refunds will be processed accordingly.
Do you offer custom material sourcing?
Yes, we can source specialized materials based on your project requirements. Contact us with details for a custom solution.
Are your materials certified?
All our materials are sourced from trusted suppliers and meet industry standards. Certifications can be provided upon request.
How do I contact your customer support team?
You can reach us via phone, email, or our website's contact form. Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
